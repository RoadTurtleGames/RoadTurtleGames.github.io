◆ ShakeState

enum MilkShake.ShakeState
strong

Represents the current state of a shake.

Enumerator
FadingIn 

The shake is starting / fading in.

Sustained 

The shake has reached its full strength and is now constant.

FadingOut 

The shake is stopping / fading out.

Stopped 

The shake has fully stopped.

◆ ShakeType

enum MilkShake.ShakeType
strong

Defines the behavior of a shake.

Enumerator
OneShot 

Will fade in and fade out automatically.

Sustained 

Will fade in automatically, but will continue to shake and will not fade out until told to stop.
