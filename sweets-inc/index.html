Welcome to Bonbonia

Welcome to Bonbonia, a land known for its insatiable appetite for all things sweet. In Sweets Inc, you'll embark on an enticing entrepreneurial journey where your goal is to captivate the palates of Bonbonians across the continent.

Creative Confectionery

Dive into the art of confectionery design by creating your own scrumptious sweets in the design studio. Use an extensive array of ingredients including nuts, fruits, berries, spices, and other flavorings to craft unique confections that are both delicious and profitable!

Upgrade and Automate

Build up your factories, progressing from humble beginnings to overseeing vast, state-of-the-art production facilities. Start with simple machines and manual workstations, then strategically upgrade to automated production lines that optimize efficiency and output.

Develop and Discover

The continent is yours to explore. Expand your business into Bonbonia's diverse regions to discover what opportunities they have to offer. Research and develop new confection-making processes and equipment to keep your business thriving.
