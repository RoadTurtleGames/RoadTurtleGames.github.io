Impact Material Registry

Note: As of Impact CFX 1.3.0, use of Impact Material Registries is no longer strictly required, but still recommended. See the section Managing Registered Materials section for details.

An Impact Material Registry is a list of Impact Materials that will be loaded into an Impact CFX Manager. On Start, the Impact CFX Manager loads all of these materials and converts them into data suitable for Jobs and Burst.

You can create a material registry by going to Assets > Create > Impact CFX > Material Registry

You can use the Scan For Impact Materials button to automatically populate the registry with all Impact Materials in your project.
