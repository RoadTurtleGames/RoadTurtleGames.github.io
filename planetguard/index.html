Welcome to the development blog for Planetguard!

What is Planetguard?

Planetguard is a sci-fi space shooter that combines aerial combat with tower-defense elements.

Customize your fighter's loadout with a wide array of weapons and support items.

Engage alien enemies in the air and on the ground in otherworldly landscapes.

Strategically manage reinforcements to help complete your objectives.
