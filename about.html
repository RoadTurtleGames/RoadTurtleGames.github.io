About Us

Road Turtle Games was created by a group of college friends and roommates who were all Computer Science students focusing in Game Design and Development. With the help of Axis Games, we managed to get our first game onto steam with relative ease. We now work on several joint projects with Axis Games as well as games on our own, and are always looking for ways to expand our reach in the gaming market.
